Our Rules & Regulations

Let’s create a harmonious learning space together. Your adherence to our rules cultivates discipline, respect, and a thriving academic environment.

Dress & Appearance

  1. Students must come to school wearing the correct school uniform. Uniform must be neatly cleaned and ironed.
  2. Personal cleanliness, hygiene and neatness is expected at all times. Hair must be neatly cut/pinned/plaited and combed. Nails must be kept clean and short. Boys must maintain their hair trim and neat.Fancy hairstyles and coloured hair is not allowed.
  3. Failure to comply may result in the student being sent home after two warnings recorded in the handbook.
  4. Students are not allowed to colour / spike / gel their hair, wear nailpolish, tattoo or any henna design. Fancy hairstyles are not allowed.
  5. The Shirt must be fully buttoned.

School Property

  1. Care must be taken to keep the school clean at all times. Litter must be disposed off in the dustbins provided.
  2. Students must take care of the school property. No scratches, writings or drawings may be made on the school furniture or walls.
  3. Damage done by accident must be reported to the class teacher. Students who willfully damage school property must make good the damage done.

School Timing

  1. Students must be present in the school at 9.45 a.m. Classes commence at 10:00 a.m.
  2. Students will not be permitted to enter class once the assembly has started at 9:45 a.m.
  3. Parents will not be allowed into the school premises after the first bell at 10:00 a.m.
  4. Students will disperse at 3:00 p.m.. Parents/Drivers coming to pick up their wards will not be permitted into the corridors/classrooms. They will have to wait in the designated areas for their wards.
  5. Children are expected to disperse after 3:00 p.m. The School will not be held resposible for any child remaining in the premises after 4:00 p.m. unless the class teacher has asked them to do so.

Behaviour

  1. Students must respect and obey their teachers and School authorities at all times.
  2. Running, playing and shouting in the classrooms and corridors during study hours is forbidden. Students must not indulge in fights and physical and verbal abuse in school. Stringent action will be taken against all students instigating and/or participating in such acts.
  3. Mobile phone, Laptop and other digital items are not allowed in School untill teacher allowed
  4. Students must record and follow the specific instructions given by their teachers.

Celebration of Birthdays

  1. Students are not permitted to wear festive dress on birthdays. They are permitted to distribute common chocolates/sweets to their classmates and teachers. However students may not enter or disturb other classes for this purpose. In this context distribution of fancy gifts / sweets & celebration of class parties is not permitted.

After – School

  1. No student is permitted to come to school on Saturdays / holidays unless the teacher has given a written note. Students coming for practices on Saturdays / holidays must be in school uniform.
  2. Parents are earnestly requested to pickup their child/ward when the school ends. Please note that school is not responsible for anything that happens after school hours unless the student is specifically called after school hours by the teachers with a written note to the effect.
